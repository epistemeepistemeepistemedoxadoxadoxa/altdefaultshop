In the past few months, Park West New York, New York has developed a series of tools that can be used to create vectors for typography and illustration as well as page layouts and video. Documentation of this process has mainly been through screenshots and screen recordings. The programs have led to many outcomes, some of which can’t be transformed into .otf or .ttf font files. I’ve decided to share some images captured during the development process to help explain what the tools are capable of and how I’ve been using them.

A demo of an early version of a simple brush tool is available here.
